Commercial

Large companies and commercial properties managers that have either grown to whole buildings or campuses in multiple locations or manage those locations see their security risks multiply accordingly. APEX3 SYSTEMS understands the unique needs of these high profile multi-faceted organizations and provides multiple solutions to specifically meet the ever evolving needs of these clients.

Schools

Whether in a neighborhood school or across a large university campus, the surveillance and security needs of those in the education market are specific and ever expanding. APEX3 SYSTEMS understands the individual challenges of educational institutions and provides cutting edge cost effective solutions to specifically meet those needs.

Manufacturing

The creation of goods presents a wide range of security challenges to protect staff and processes as well as monitor inventory. APEX3 SYSTEMS understands the unique needs of manufacturers and provides custom cost effective solutions to specifically meet those needs.

Retail

In large department stores or the corner market, all retailers face increasing loss from shoppers and staff alike. APEX3 SYSTEMS understands these risks and provides cost effective technology solutions to mitigate those risks and offer piece of mind to you and staff.

Municipalties

From the largest city to the most rural community, municipalities face the challenge of protecting citizens and property with limited resources and constrained budgets. APEX3 SYSTEMS understands the unique needs of municipalities and provides cost effective solutions to specifically address those needs.

Logistics

Goods fuel the global economy. The loading, unloading and transit of goods require advanced and cost effective security solutions. APEX3 SYSTEMS understands the needs of the transportation and warehousing industry and provides cutting edge solutions to specifically meet those needs.
